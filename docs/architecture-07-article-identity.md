# Article Identity — Sourced Knowledge

## The Problem

An article on YoTop10 must serve a specific purpose that Medium doesn't: it's a fact mine.

## The Fix

Articles should have:
- **Source citations** (not optional — required for articles)
- **Fact-check status** (unverified / verified / disputed — by admin)
- **Revision visibility** (articles can be updated, changelog shows diffs)
- **Related rankings** (links to Top 10 posts on the same topic)

This makes YoTop10 articles different from Medium blogs. They're sourced knowledge pieces, not opinion posts. This aligns with the Wikipedia influence.
